About Turn it on

Finding the light switch in the dark is a blind folded game usually ending with your pinky toe hitting the edge of something. “Turn it on” is an app that would connect to the blue lights and switch in your house, turning them on before you enter a room, saving your toes from being crushed. You also no longer have to have to fumble for keys in your bag, the app would be able to connect to Bluetooth locks and alarm system on your house and car, locking and unlocking them as you enter and leave. “Turn it on” is great for those who are always in a rush or can never find their keys in their bag, so everyone. Bluetooth locks, light switch, lightbulbs, and other similar products have been around for a while, but they still require you to unlock it from your phone. “Turn it on” gives you hands free access to your home, car, and other Bluetooth enabled devices near you.


How it Works

Using the already available Bluetooth locks, light switch, and lightbulbs, “Turn it on” works by connecting the local network of your house or other saved locations in order to access and turn them on and off. The security of this app was important when planning it, ensuring that it cannot be hacked due to what will be connected through the app. Restricting the app to have to connect to the network to gain access to the connect Bluetooth devices means that the app cannot control switches must have the username and password of that network.
